# AFIFinder
AFI Finder Privacy Policy
Last Updated: March 2026

Overview
DAFI Text Finder (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.
DAFI Text Finder is designed to function entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The App:

Does not require user accounts

Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information

Does not collect location data

Does not collect device identifiers

Does not use analytics services

Does not use tracking technologies

Does not display third-party advertisements

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

User-created notes

Bookmarks

Search index data

App preferences

This information:

Is stored only on your device

Is not transmitted to any server

Is not accessible by the developer

If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Exported Notes

Users may export notes as a file to the Apple Files app (On My iPhone or iCloud Drive).

Any exported files are managed entirely by the user and are subject to Apple’s iCloud and Files privacy policies if stored in iCloud.

The App does not receive or store exported data.

Internet Connectivity

The App does not require an internet connection to function once installed. It does not transmit usage data or document access information.

Third-Party Services

DAFI Text Finder does not integrate with third-party analytics providers, advertising networks, or external data services.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact: stitchtechnologies@protonmail.com
